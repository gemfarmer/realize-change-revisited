About Us

Dream Think Tank is a crowd-sourced and community-led project that aims to create a space where people of all ages and backgrounds can dream about their future and share a conversation about their dreams and hopes with others.

Mission:

This project utilizes insights from psychology, sociology and the latest in technology to develop a platform for community engaged research on future oriented cognition where people can dream, aspire, and create a better future together.
