About Us

RealizeChange is a crowd-sourced and crowd-funded project that provides individuals and institutional partners with the tools necessary to:

  • amplify the voice of the people
  • open lines of communication
  • collaboratively build our shared dreams and goals

Mission:

The mission of this project is to build an interactive, incentivized website that allows people from all walks of life to participate in a plan for the future. This project will dynamically evolve with time as its goals are better integrated and informed by participants. The website will aim to accomplish three objectives: voice peoples dreams and goals for the future, visualize and learn about the dreams of others, and incentivize collaboration and project building.

The ultimate goal of this project is to be accessible and fun for people of ALL backgrounds and to empower them to Dream and co-create an equitable and representative plan for our future. Join us on this journey.
